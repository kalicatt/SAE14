About me

A lover of photography and sportst

my name is lucas, I'm 18 years old (soon 19 in january). It's been several months now that I could recover by chance, one of the last professional cameras of my father. I then discovered a quality as a photographer. Since then, photography is an integral part of my life, I practice it daily, in my sports, and even at the university! of the university library. And as I mentioned earlier I even integrate photography in my sport, volleyball. And yes in addition to being a photographer, I am a sportsman, it is a second religion for me, I could not imagine my life without sports, especially volleyball. I've been practicing this sport for 2 years now, and I've done a lot of things during this time : French championships, 4x4, tournaments, etc... In short I will stop throwing flowers at me and let you continue the visit, you will find my work as a photographer, in my sport as well as daily life.

Lucas

Don't worry I have no regrets and I love my life as it is now!
